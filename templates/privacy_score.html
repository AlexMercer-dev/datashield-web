Understanding Your Privacy Score

How DataShield helps you measure and improve your online privacy

What is a Privacy Score?

Your Privacy Score is a numerical representation of how well your online activities are protected from tracking and data collection. DataShield calculates this score based on multiple factors, giving you a clear picture of your privacy status.

The score ranges from 0 to 100:

0-40: Poor privacy protection. Your data is likely being collected by multiple trackers.
41-70: Fair protection. Some measures in place, but significant room for improvement.
71-90: Good protection. Most tracking attempts are being blocked.
91-100: Excellent protection. Your browsing is highly secure and private.

Factors That Affect Your Score

Tracker Blocking

How effectively you're blocking tracking scripts, pixels, and beacons across websites.

Cookie Management

How well you're controlling which cookies are stored and for how long.

Fingerprinting Protection

How well you're preventing websites from identifying you through browser fingerprinting.

Browsing History

How frequently you clear your browsing history and cached data.

Search Engine

Whether you're using privacy-focused search engines that don't track your searches.

Social Media Integration

How connected your browsing is to social media platforms that track your activity.

Your Privacy Dashboard

DataShield provides a comprehensive dashboard that shows your current Privacy Score and breaks down each contributing factor.

The dashboard updates in real-time as you browse, giving you immediate feedback on how your actions affect your privacy. You can also see a history of your score over time, helping you track your progress.
