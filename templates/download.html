Installation Guide

Chrome Installation

  1. Click the "Download for Chrome" button above
  2. When prompted, click "Add to Chrome"
  3. Confirm the installation by clicking "Add extension"
  4. Once installed, you'll see the DataShield icon in your browser toolbar
  5. Click the icon to access DataShield's dashboard and settings

Firefox Installation

  1. Click the "Download for Firefox" button above
  2. When prompted, click "Add to Firefox"
  3. Confirm the installation by clicking "Add"
  4. Grant the necessary permissions when asked
  5. Access DataShield from the icon in your browser toolbar
